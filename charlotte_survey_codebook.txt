=== DATA GENERATION NOTES ===

- County samples range from 200 (minimum) to 1,182 (Mecklenburg)
- Distributions based on:
  * Census demographic data for Charlotte metro
  * Well-being research (life satisfaction ~6.8 mean, SD ~2)
  * Income inequality by race/ethnicity
  * Urban-rural differences in safety perception, belonging, housing concerns
  * Age-related employment and income patterns
  * Correlations between well-being, income, employment, social support

- Key correlations embedded:
  * Life satisfaction ↔ income (r≈0.35)
  * Life satisfaction ↔ employment status (unemployed lower)
  * Trust ↔ life satisfaction (r≈0.40)
  * Social support ↔ life satisfaction (r≈0.45)
  * Housing concern ↔ county (urban higher)
  * Belonging ↔ county type (rural/small town stronger)
